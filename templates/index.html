Account Deletion Instructions

CCTV Alerts allows users to delete their accounts and all associated data directly from within the app. Follow the steps below to permanently remove your account:

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the CCTV Alerts app.
  2. Go to the Settings page.
  3. Scroll down to the Delete Account section.
  4. Tap "Delete My Account".
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Once confirmed, your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted from our system. This action cannot be undone.
